## Purpose

Track the work required before a candidate can enter the verified Paper
Library.

## Planned states

1. Candidate captured.
2. Primary sources located.
3. Metadata verified.
4. Update rule and assumptions extracted.
5. Evidence protocol reviewed.
6. Human note ready.
7. Promoted to the Paper Library.

## Verification rules

Queue membership is not evidence of publication, acceptance, relevance, or
correctness. Each status transition must be supported by a recorded review
action.
