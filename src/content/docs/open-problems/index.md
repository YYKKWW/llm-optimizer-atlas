## Scaffold status

This directory is ready for problem notes, but no research statement has been
promoted into the atlas.

## Purpose

Turn promising questions into reviewable problem definitions rather than loose
lists of ideas.

## Planned problem record

- Precise question and mathematical setting.
- Assumptions, feasible set, and stationarity notion.
- Known evidence and missing evidence.
- Relationship to verified paper records.
- Candidate proof, counterexample, or experiment.
- Completion and falsification criteria.
- Human-maintained notes.

## Verification rules

Known results must be sourced. Conjectures, interpretations, and empirical
hypotheses must be labeled as such and must not be presented as established
facts.
