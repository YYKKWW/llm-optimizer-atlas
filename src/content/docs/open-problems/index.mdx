Every record below labels its origin as either a source-stated problem or an
atlas synthesis grounded in precise locations from the verified paper corpus.
A record being “verified” means its metadata, cited boundary, and open-status
rationale were checked; it does not mean that the question has been solved.

## Promotion boundary

Scheduled discovery produces unverified candidate artifacts only. A candidate
enters this index only after a human review supplies exact source locations,
scope and assumptions, a reason the boundary remains open, and explicit
resolution and falsification criteria.
