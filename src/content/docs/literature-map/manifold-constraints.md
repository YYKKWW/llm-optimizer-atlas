## Purpose

Study explicit matrix constraints through projections, retractions, tangent and
normal geometry, nonsmooth spectral sets, and constrained stationarity.

## Questions this track will answer

- What changes when a leading singular value is simple or repeated?
- Which tangent object and stationarity notion match the feasible set?
- When is an approximate spectral direction a valid oracle output?
- How do projected, retracted, and branch-switching updates differ?
- Under what conditions can explicit constraints be compared with weight decay?

## Evidence standard
