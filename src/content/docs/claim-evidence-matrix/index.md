## Scaffold status

No research claim or supporting result is recorded yet.

## Purpose

Prevent slogans from replacing conditional conclusions by tying every claim to
its evidence, comparison conditions, and limitations.

## Planned fields

- Claim text and claim owner.
- Evidence type and primary source.
- Model size and architecture context.
- Token, FLOP, compute, and wall-clock budgets.
- Baseline and tuning protocol.
- Evaluation metric and uncertainty.
- Optimizer-state and systems costs.
- Known limitations, counterevidence, and verification date.

## Verification rules

A claim cannot be described as supported until the evidence record is complete
enough to reconstruct the comparison conditions. Mathematical interpretation
must be labeled separately from an author's stated claim.

## Next step
