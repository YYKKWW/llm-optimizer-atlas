## Purpose

Explain why apparently conflicting conclusions may arise without collapsing
them into a ranking of optimizers.

## Comparison protocol

Each entry will align the claim, optimizer definition, model scale, data and
training budget, baseline tuning, metric, wall-clock treatment, systems cost,
and statistical uncertainty. It will then separate direct contradiction from
differences in scope or protocol.

## Planned outcomes

- Resolved by a documented protocol difference.
- Still conflicting under comparable conditions.
- Insufficient evidence for comparison.
- Candidate open problem or discriminating experiment.

## Verification rules

This page will link to canonical paper and claim records instead of duplicating
metadata or paraphrasing unsupported conclusions.
