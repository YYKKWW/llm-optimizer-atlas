Inspect the repository and read AGENTS.md.

Generate the open-problem index and one durable detail route per verified
open-problem record.

Required page content:
- status, type, origin, research tracks, tags, and verification date;
- precise question, scope, and assumptions;
- known results and conditions;
- exact paper source versions and locators;
- unknown and verification boundary;
- why the problem remains open;
- candidate approaches;
- resolution and falsification criteria;
- links to atlas paper records, arXiv reading pages, and formal versions.

Pages must load formal data at build time and must not duplicate problem or
paper metadata manually. The generator may write only inside its owned
generated directory, must detect drift and path escape, and must never change
human_notes or hand-written files.

Add tests and run validation, link checks, and the production build.
