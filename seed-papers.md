## Verification rules

For every paper:

- Use primary sources.
- Do not infer acceptance or publication from author wording.
- Record all titles exactly.
- Store arXiv version and verification date.
- Record the exact optimizer update when available.
- Separate paper claims from our interpretation.
- Do not add numerical performance claims without model size, training budget,
  baseline, and metric.
- Mark uncertain fields `TODO_UNVERIFIED`.
